Kick the day off with adventure

Explore the waterfalls and caves of the local paradise of the Plitvice Lakes National Park, declared a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The main attractions of this park, unique in the world, are the 16 small lakes joined by waterfalls created by the sedimentation of travertine, a special type of limestone.

A route of over 1,000 islands

With 1,244 islands, islets and crags to be exact, and every one of them surrounded by crystal clear waters, it’s time to come aboard! Island hopping is undoubtedly the best way to discover the Croatian coast from a truly dazzling perspective.

The best recipe to enjoy the trip

Refill your batteries, recharge your energy with the exquisite local cooking, traditional delicacies and fine wines before strolling around Diocletian’s magnificent palace.
